Davao City, Philippines

The Philippines is an archipelagic country in Southeast Asia, located in the western Pacific Ocean. It consists of about 7,641 islands, categorized broadly into three main geographical divisions: Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. The capital city is Manila, with Quezon City being the most populous city. The country has a rich cultural heritage influenced by its history as a colony of Spain for over 300 years, followed by American occupation. This blend is evident in its diverse culture, languages (with Filipino and English as official languages), and traditions.
